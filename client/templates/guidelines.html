Scoring Guidelines

  • 4 points for an exact scoreline prediction (e.g., 2-1 predicted, and actual score is 2-1).
  • 2 points for correct result + correct score for the one team team.
  • 1 point for correct result but no correct team score.
  • 0 points for incorrect result, invalid pick. 1-0/0-1 picks are considered invalid unless the final score is also 1-0/0-1, in which case 4 points are awarded.
