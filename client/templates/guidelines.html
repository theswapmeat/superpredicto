Scoring Guidelines

  • 4 points for an exact scoreline prediction (e.g., 2-1 predicted, and actual score is 2-1).
  • 2 points for correct result + correct score for the winning team (e.g., predicted 3-1, actual 2-1).
  • 1 point for correct result but neither team’s score is exact (e.g., predicted 1-0, actual 3-2).
  • 0 points for incorrect result or invalid pick.
    Note: Predictions of 1-0 or 0-1 are considered invalid unless the actual score is also exactly 1-0 or 0-1 — in which case, 4 points are awarded.
