How Scoring Works

Scoring Guidelines

Points are awarded per match based on how close your predicted scoreline is to the final result.

Points per prediction

4 pts

Exact scoreline prediction (e.g., you predict 2-1 and the actual score is 2-1).

2 pts

Correct result + correct score for the winning team (e.g., you predict 3-1, actual is 2-1).

1 pt

Correct result, but neither team's score is exact (e.g., you predict 1-0, actual is 3-2).

0 pts

Incorrect result or invalid pick.
Note: Predictions of 1-0 or 0-1 are considered invalid unless the actual score is also exactly 1-0 or 0-1 — in which case 4 points are awarded.

Examples

Actual Score Your Prediction Points Reason
2 – 1 2 – 1 4 Perfect pick

3 – 1 0 – 1 0 Invalid pick — a 0-1 prediction counts as invalid unless it's an exact match
1 – 0 1 – 0 4 Valid 1-0 pick (exact match)
1 – 1 2 – 2 1 Correct result (draw), but no exact scores
