How to read this

Green = what players predicted · Gold = what actually happened — each shown as a share of all picks / all games. When the gold bar is taller than the green one, that result happened more often than the crowd expected; when green is taller, the crowd over-predicted it.

The dashed line is the smooth shape the predicted bars roughly follow — a bell curve for goal margins, a right-skewed curve for total goals. It's a visual guide only, not extra data.
